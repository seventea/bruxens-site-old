Bruxens

Back in 2013, two friends with distinct expertise decided to transform a vintage garage store into a Screenprint workshop. One known as "the ink" and the other as "the scissor hand", Apostolis and Andreas created the vintage line known as Bruxens.

Born and raised in the city centers of Brussels and Athens, they share a passion for music, bikes and old-fashion styles. The inevitable clash of vintage inspiration and modern city life impelled them to escape to the countryside.Â
